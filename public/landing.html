Common questions

Kya mujhe technical knowledge chahiye?
+

Bilkul nahi. The GuestInn ek simple mobile-first app hai. Agar aap WhatsApp use kar sakte ho, toh yeh bhi easily use kar sakte ho. Humara onboarding team puri setup mein help karta hai.

Kya multiple hotels manage kar sakte hain?
+

Haan, Enterprise plan mein aap ek hi account se multiple properties manage kar sakte ho. Har hotel ka apna alag dashboard, data aur staff access hota hai.

Kya guest data safe hai?
+

Haan. Humara platform enterprise-grade encryption ke saath aata hai. Har hotel ka data isolated hota hai — koi dusra hotel aapka data nahi dekh sakta.

Free trial kab tak hai?
+

Pro plan ka 14-day free trial hai — credit card ki zarurat nahi. Trial ke baad aap decide karo. Cancel karne par koi charges nahi.

AI Scanner kaunse ID types support karta hai?
+

Aadhaar Card, PAN Card, Passport, Driving License, Voter ID — sab support hote hain. Foreign passports bhi scan kiye ja sakte hain with 99% accuracy.
